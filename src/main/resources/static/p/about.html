About Etext.store

Etext.store is a repository of prestigious & referrable texts.

What makes Etext.store special is the facility to reference any bit of text of its collections.

Not only can you make a reference to a work, but any part of it, even that special special paragraph in the middle of the book!

As such, not only do we offer pretty, common sense, stable URLs, like https://textbase.scriptorium.ro/cosbuc/balade_si_idile/rea_de_plata but you can quote any part of any work, be it a section, paragraph, verse or any arbitrary chunk of text really.

To see how seriously we take referencing, on Textbase you can actually reference a letter of a work using a unique URL that will never change.

As such, Textbase is the ultimate database of the wisdom that you can share and refer chapter&verse, literally to the, hum, letter.

That makes Textbase the perfect source for sharing & social networking use of of prestigious text. Read a classic for school and quote it on facebook!

Yes, we're big.
